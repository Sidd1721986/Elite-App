Our Purpose

Mission Statement

The goal of Elite Home Services is to be the Homeowner's Advocate. We consolidate and replace dozens of confusing options for maintenance and updates, from roofs and windows to plumbing and electrical, even kitchen and bath. You can trust our vetted vendors for your home. We vet and partner with the top companies across dozens of categories to provide you with a single place to access services from qualified, trusted vendors at the right price.

Whatever your needs, just let us know with one central request and we send the correlating professionals out — fast, simple, headache free. No fees, no picking three random vendors and spending hours researching. Need multiple things done? Simply place a single request and let us handle it.

We are revolutionizing the home services industry. Whether you're a Homeowner, Realtor, Landlord, or Property Manager — simply visit us in-app or online. We've got your home covered.

Thank you for allowing us to serve you, for telling your friends and family, and God Bless America!
